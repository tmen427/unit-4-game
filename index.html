Crystal Collector Game

You will recieve a random target number at the start of the game. There are 4 crystals below. Each crystal also contains a random number, but you don't know what it is until you click on them. You can click on each crystals as many times as you like and they will add up. You win the game by clicking on the crystals and matching it with the random target number. If your points goes over the random number you lose and the game starts over.
